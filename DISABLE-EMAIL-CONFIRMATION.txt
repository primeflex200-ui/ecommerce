HOW TO DISABLE EMAIL CONFIRMATION IN SUPABASE
==============================================

To allow users to sign in immediately without email confirmation:

1. Go to your Supabase Dashboard:
   https://blsgyybaevuytmgpljyk.supabase.co

2. Click on "Authentication" in the left sidebar

3. Click on "Providers" tab

4. Find "Email" provider and click on it

5. Scroll down to find "Confirm email" toggle

6. TURN OFF the "Confirm email" toggle

7. Click "Save" button

After this, new users can sign in immediately without confirming their email.

IMPORTANT: Users who already signed up will still need to confirm their email.
To manually confirm existing users:
1. Go to Authentication > Users
2. Find the user
3. Click on the user
4. Click "Confirm email" button

OR you can delete the unconfirmed user and have them sign up again.
